## Ingredients

- 8% rye
- 12% whole wheat
- 12% bread flour
- 68% AP flour
- 365g water
- 11g salt (dissolved coarse salt)

## Schedule

- **1:15am** Feed starter
- **10:15am** Mix flour, water, most water
- **11am** Add remaining water and salt
- **11:30am** Fold
- **12:15pm** Fold
- **1:15pm** Fold
- **5:30pm** Shape, cold proof in the fridge
- **~2pm next day** Bake

## Notes

Much better fermentation from waiting an extra hour! Should go even 30 mins longer next time.
